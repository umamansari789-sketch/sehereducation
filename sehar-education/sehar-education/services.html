🇬🇧 Study in United Kingdom

World-class universities, diverse culture, post-study work visa opportunities

← Back to Home

Why Study in the UK?

The United Kingdom offers some of the world's most prestigious universities, including Oxford, Cambridge, UCL, and Imperial College London. With a rich academic tradition spanning centuries, UK degrees are globally recognised and highly valued by employers worldwide.

Most undergraduate programs are 3 years and postgraduate programs are just 1 year, making it cost-effective compared to other English-speaking countries. The Post-Study Work (PSW) visa allows international graduates to stay and work in the UK for 2 years after graduation (3 years for PhD students).

📋 Key Facts

Tuition Fees£10,000 – £38,000/yr
Living Costs£12,000 – £15,000/yr
IELTS Required6.0 – 7.5+
Visa TypeStudent Route Visa
Post-Study Work2 Years (PSW)
